Our Activities

We offer a diverse range of activities designed to develop both practical skills and digital literacy. Our programs balance outdoor adventures, life skills, and technology education to create well-rounded individuals who can thrive in the AI era.

Tent Building

Duration: 1.5 hours

Learn how to set up different types of tents and shelters, understanding the importance of proper site selection and tent orientation for various weather conditions.

Knot Tying

Duration: 1 hour

Master essential knots for outdoor activities, including the bowline, clove hitch, and taut-line hitch, understanding when and how to use each knot effectively.

Tarp Setup

Duration: 1 hour

Learn various tarp configurations for different weather conditions and purposes, from simple sun shades to complex rain shelters.

Nature Exploration

Duration: 2 hours

Guided bushwalks to identify local flora and fauna, learn navigation skills, and understand the principles of Leave No Trace outdoor ethics.

First Aid Basics

Duration: 1.5 hours

Learn essential first aid skills including treating minor injuries, recognizing emergency situations, and understanding when and how to seek help.

Team Building Games

Duration: 1 hour

Engage in fun, collaborative activities designed to build trust, improve communication, and develop problem-solving skills in a group setting.

Outdoor Cooking

Duration: 2 hours

Learn to prepare simple, nutritious meals using outdoor cooking methods, understanding food safety and meal planning for outdoor activities.

Computer Basics

Duration: 1.5 hours

Introduction to computer hardware, software, and operating systems, with hands-on practice in file management, internet navigation, and basic troubleshooting.

Digital Collaboration Tools

Duration: 1.5 hours

Learn to use cloud-based collaboration tools like Google Workspace and Microsoft 365, understanding how to share documents, collaborate in real-time, and manage digital projects.

Website Building

Duration: 2 hours

Introduction to website creation using user-friendly platforms like Wix or WordPress, learning about design principles, content organization, and basic HTML/CSS concepts.

AI Exploration

Duration: 1.5 hours

Introduction to artificial intelligence concepts and applications, with hands-on experience using AI tools like ChatGPT, DALL-E, and Midjourney for creative and educational purposes.

Responsible AI Use

Duration: 1 hour

Learn about ethical considerations in AI use, including privacy, bias, and responsible consumption of AI-generated content, with practical guidelines for safe and effective AI tool use.
