Module Details

Our curriculum is divided into several key modules, each focusing on different aspects of outdoor skills and digital literacy. These modules are designed to complement each other and provide a well-rounded educational experience.

Outdoor Skills Module

This module focuses on developing practical outdoor skills that foster self-reliance, teamwork, and a connection with nature.

  • Tent building and campsite setup
  • Knot tying and rope skills
  • Basic navigation and map reading
  • Outdoor cooking techniques
  • First aid and safety awareness
  • Team-building activities

Digital Literacy Module

This module introduces children to essential digital skills and tools, preparing them for the increasingly digital world.

  • Computer fundamentals and internet safety
  • Digital collaboration tools (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365)
  • Introduction to AI tools and responsible use
  • Basic website building
  • Digital content creation
  • Online communication skills

Life Skills Module

This module focuses on developing essential life skills that will serve children well in all aspects of their lives.

  • Problem-solving and critical thinking
  • Communication and collaboration
  • Time management and organization
  • Adaptability and resilience
  • Leadership and initiative
  • Creativity and innovation

Program Format

Our programs are structured to provide a balanced and engaging learning experience. We offer both holiday programs and regular after-school sessions to accommodate different schedules and preferences.

Holiday Programs

Intensive, immersive experiences during school holidays that allow for deeper exploration of topics and more extensive outdoor activities.

  • Full-day programs (9:00 AM - 3:00 PM)
  • 5-day duration (Monday to Friday)
  • Comprehensive curriculum covering all modules
  • Field trips and special activities
  • Small group sizes (maximum 12 children per group)
  • Age-appropriate grouping (7-9, 10-12, 13-15)

After-School Programs

Regular weekly sessions during the school term that provide consistent skill development and reinforcement.

  • 2-hour sessions (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM)
  • Once or twice weekly options
  • 10-week term structure
  • Focused modules with progressive skill development
  • Small group sizes (maximum 10 children per group)
  • Age-appropriate grouping (7-9, 10-12, 13-15)

Key Differences

What sets our programs apart from traditional educational approaches is our balanced focus on both outdoor skills and digital literacy, combined with our emphasis on practical, hands-on learning.

Balanced Approach

Unlike programs that focus exclusively on either outdoor activities or technology, we provide a balanced curriculum that recognizes the importance of both. This approach helps children develop a diverse set of skills and a more holistic understanding of the world.

Practical Learning

We emphasize hands-on, experiential learning rather than theoretical instruction. Children learn by doing, which enhances retention and makes the learning process more engaging and enjoyable.

Small Group Sizes

We maintain small group sizes to ensure that each child receives personalized attention and support. This allows us to tailor our approach to individual learning styles and needs, maximizing the effectiveness of our programs.

Qualified Instructors

Our instructors are experienced educators with expertise in both outdoor education and digital technology. They are passionate about helping children develop the skills they need to thrive in the modern world.
