Winter Holiday Program 2023

Join us this winter for an exciting blend of technology, creativity, and outdoor activities. Our program is designed to help children develop essential digital skills while maintaining a healthy balance with physical activities and social interaction.

Our Winter Holiday Program runs from July 10-14, 2023, from 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM daily. The program is designed for children aged 8-14 and includes a balanced mix of technology workshops, creative projects, and outdoor activities.

Parent Information

What to Bring

  • Lunch and snacks (no nuts please)
  • Water bottle
  • Hat and sunscreen
  • Comfortable clothing and closed shoes
  • Laptop (optional - we provide equipment)

Important Notes

  • Drop-off: 8:30-9:00 AM, Pick-up: 3:30-4:00 PM
  • Extended care available (additional fee)
  • All staff have Working with Children Checks
  • First aid trained staff on-site
  • Please inform us of any allergies or special needs

Daily Schedule

Day 1: Computer Fundamentals & Digital Citizenship

Morning

Computer Basics

Introduction to computer hardware, software, and operating systems. Hands-on exploration of different devices and their components.

Digital Citizenship

Learning about online safety, privacy, and responsible digital behavior through interactive activities and discussions.

Afternoon

Team Building Games

Fun outdoor activities designed to help students get to know each other and develop teamwork skills.

Digital Art Basics

Introduction to digital drawing tools and techniques. Students will create their first digital artwork.

Day 2: AI Exploration & Creative Thinking

Morning

AI Fundamentals

Introduction to artificial intelligence concepts through age-appropriate examples and interactive demonstrations.

AI Art Creation

Exploring AI-assisted art tools to create unique digital artwork with guidance on prompt engineering.

Afternoon

Creative Problem Solving

Engaging in design thinking activities that encourage innovative approaches to everyday challenges.

Outdoor STEM Challenges

Participating in hands-on outdoor activities that apply science and engineering principles in fun ways.

Day 3: Collaboration Tools & Team Projects

Morning

Digital Collaboration

Learning to use online collaboration tools like Google Docs, Slides, and digital whiteboards for team projects.

Project Planning

Introduction to project management concepts and tools. Students will plan their team projects.

Afternoon

Team Project Kickoff

Students begin working on their team projects, applying the collaboration tools they learned in the morning.

Outdoor Team Challenges

Physical activities that reinforce teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills.

Day 4: Web Design & Digital Storytelling

Morning

Web Design Basics

Introduction to HTML and CSS through user-friendly platforms. Students will learn about web page structure and styling.

Building a Simple Website

Hands-on workshop where students create their first web page using drag-and-drop tools and basic code.

Afternoon

Digital Storytelling

Learning to create engaging digital stories using multimedia tools, combining text, images, and audio.

Team Project Work

Continued work on team projects with a focus on incorporating web design and storytelling elements.

Day 5: Project Completion & Showcase

Morning

Project Finalization

Teams complete their projects, adding final touches and preparing for presentation.

Presentation Skills

Workshop on effective presentation techniques and public speaking, with practice sessions.

Afternoon

Project Showcase

Students present their team projects to the group, demonstrating what they've learned and created.

Celebration & Certificates

Closing ceremony with certificate presentation, reflection activities, and celebration of achievements.
