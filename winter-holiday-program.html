Winter Holiday Program Schedule

This session is tailor-made for kids and youth, using a kid-friendly approach to make learning fun and engaging. Many activities will be presented by Melvin, a well-loved star Scout patrol leader who is a hit with our students!

Last Minute Promotion!

A$9.90 per half-day session

Original Price: A$39.90

You can register for any one or more sessions to suit your schedule!

Instructions for Parents

AM Session: Drop off before 8am or after 9am. Pick up at 1pm.

PM Session: Drop off at 1pm. Pick up at 5pm.

We provide a sandwich for lunch, but you are welcome to bring your own.

Detailed instructions will be provided upon registration. Chess, card, and board games will be available between activities.

Monday

AM: Computer Fundamentals
  • Understand hardware, software, files, and applications.
  • Learn about the internet, email, websites, and apps.
  • Hands-on: Install apps and manage files.
PM: Digital Skills
  • Organize with Email, schedule with Calendar, and manage files with Google Drive.
  • Hands-on practice with each tool.

Tuesday

AM: AI Exploration
  • Explore ChatGPT for ideas and Claude for summarizing.
  • Hands-on: Build your own private math tutor with AI.
PM: Build Your First AI App
  • Get an introduction to the PartyRock platform.
  • Hands-on: Build a simple but functional AI app.

Wednesday

AM: Collaboration Tools
  • Learn to use Miro for collaborative projects.
  • Understand how to manage tasks with Jira.
PM: Host a Meeting
  • Learn the essentials of hosting a Zoom meeting.
  • Understand how to host a Google Meeting.

Thursday

AM: Online Marketplaces
  • Learn how to safely buy and sell on Gumtree.
  • Explore buying and selling on Facebook Marketplace.
PM: Build Your Website
  • Get a hands-on introduction to web development and build a personal website.

Friday

AM: Build Your Own Store
  • Learn the basics of e-commerce and create your own online store.
PM: Build Your YouTube Channel
  • From content creation to publishing, learn the steps to launch a YouTube channel.
